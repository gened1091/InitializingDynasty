Simulating a Legalist Empire: Game Instructions


You are the emperor of a new 25-person dynasty that is in danger of falling in anarchy without a leader to organize society.

Your empire is split into 3 districts: agriculture, trade, and office; however, not every citizen is equally skilled in these three areas, and they are initially assigned to these fields at random. You need to match people to their abilities, as this will affect how much money your society is able to make per round.

What you must decide:

To help advise you in your decisions, you have the option of investing in increased surveillance, which helps you discover more about your citizens and your districts. Indeed, the values that you are presented for each citizen (as shown above) are NOT accurate: they each contain some sampling error! Upgrading your surveillance capabilities will reduce this error and allow you to make more informed decisions, but will also cost significant amounts of money, potentially putting your society in dire financial straits if you aren't careful. There are FIVE levels of surveillance that you can choose from, and each upgrade is a one-time purchase in the game.

What you must decide:

Your tasks are to increase wealth, mitigate unrest, and optimize district efficiency. As you accomplish these three tasks, your optimization score will increase. How fast can you reach 100% optimization?
